Exploring the Dynamic Characteristics of Emotional Propagation Using a Sentiment Network Model

Home

In the age of social media, people share their emotions and thoughts through platforms like Twitter and Weibo. These emotional expressions not only reflect individual emotional states but also influence the emotional experiences and behaviors of others. This article introduces a method for exploring the dynamic characteristics of emotional propagation using a sentiment network model, providing new perspectives and methods for understanding emotional propagation in social media.

Firstly, we need to build a sentiment network model to describe the propagation and evolution of emotional information in social media. In this model, users and emotional information are treated as nodes in the network, while the social relationships between users and emotional propagation relationships are treated as edges. Through this network model, we can intuitively understand the propagation paths and influence of emotional information in social media.

Next, we can use this sentiment network model to analyze the dynamic characteristics of emotional propagation in social media. We can study the temporal trends of emotional information, analyze the impact of different events or topics on emotional propagation, and understand the clustering patterns and evolution laws of emotions in social networks. This helps us gain a deeper understanding of the mechanisms and rules of emotional propagation in social media.

Lastly, we can combine the sentiment network model with other analysis methods, such as community detection algorithms and emotional influence analysis, to further improve our understanding and prediction capabilities for emotional propagation in social media. Through this method, we can better grasp the dynamic characteristics of emotional propagation in social media, providing more accurate analysis and prediction for mental health monitoring and social event forecasting.

In conclusion, exploring the dynamic characteristics of emotional propagation using a sentiment network model will provide us with new research perspectives and methods, helping us gain a deeper understanding of the mechanisms and influence of emotional propagation in social media.
